<p>Birmingham Color Graphics is a full service printing facility located in Birmingham Alabama.  The firm was established in &quot;1991&quot; as a service bureau providing high resolution scanning and postscript film to the trade. Today the company has grown to be a place where you can have almost anything printed. Our combination of digital &amp; conventional production equipment, provides you the options needed to produce your  project on time and on budget. Birmingham Color Graphics gives you the convenience of being a single source solution for commercial printing, large format printing, outdoor  printing and apparel printing.The company also provides a full line of finishing options such as coil bindery, mounting, routing and lamination.  The quality control and flexability these services provide keep you competitive in todays color critical and deadline oriented world. </p>
